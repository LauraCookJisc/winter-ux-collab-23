Welcome to Licence subscriptions manager

Licence subscriptions manager is an online tool developed by Jisc that enables institutions to search, purchase, and manage their subscriptions to digital content. This includes access to e-books, e-journals, and databases negotiated by Jisc Collections.

Jisc Collections negotiates with publishers at a national level to procure and license affordable digital content for the UK education and research community.

You can also use Licence subscriptions manager to manage access to other selected Jisc services, including JUSP and e-books for FE.

Use the search box above to browse the Catalogue to find out what digital content is available for your institution. You can also explore the latest products below.

To find out more about Licence subscriptions manager, please visit our about pages.

Related services

Jisc Collections

Jisc Collections is the negotiation and licensing service that supports the procurement of digital content for higher and further education and research institutions in the UK.

Chest

Chest delivers trusted, value-for-money licences for software and online resources.

 

KB+

KB+ (Knowledge Base+) is a shared community service that helps UK academic libraries manage their electronic resources more efficiently.

JUSP

JUSP (Journal Usage Statistics Portal) is a shared portal that enables efficient access to e-resource usage statistics.
